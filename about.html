From curiosity to shipping real products

I started building because I wanted to create things that did not exist yet. That curiosity took me from learning code on my own to publishing Flying Up on Steam, building an AI forecasting platform at hamdiai.com, and co-developing an LSTM-based patent application (No: 2026/004897).

Today, I study in the IB Diploma Programme at SEV American College and focus on computer science, AI, and applied mathematics. I also do independent research and technical writing about model design, overfitting, black-box systems, and AI ethics in finance.

I care about building systems that are not only accurate, but also explainable and useful for real users. My process is simple: build, test hard, document honestly, and iterate fast.
